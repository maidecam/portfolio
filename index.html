Education

Saint Joseph French High School

Lycée Saint Joseph is a private French high school located in Izmir, Turkey. The school was founded by Lasalle Institute in 1880. Classes are taught in Turkish, French and English.

Özyeğin University

Özyeğin University is the 5th most successful foundation university in Turkey according to Times Higher Education. The school aims to serve society as an entrepreneurial research university by creating, sharing, and applying solution-oriented, high value-added knowledge.

L'Ecole Spéciale d'Architecture (ESA)

L'Ecole Spéciale d'Architecture (ESA) is a higher education institution of architecture in Paris. It is the only private school allowing to register with the order of the architect, and the oldest school of architecture in France (1865).
